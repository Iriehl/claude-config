Do NOT recommend remarketing, site visitor audiences, YouTube engaged audiences, or any behavioural audience signals for Contrelle's YouTube or Demand Gen campaigns.

**Why:** GMC account is flagged under Personalised Advertising — Personal Hardships. Using behavioural audiences tied to incontinence-related browsing/viewing implicitly targets people by health condition, which violates the restriction. A previous Claude session gave this advice without applying the policy constraint — it was wrong.

**How to apply:** For Demand Gen and YouTube, always recommend contextual targeting and custom intent (keyword-based) only. Flag optimised targeting as needing close monitoring. Never suggest remarketing lists or YouTube engaged audiences for this account.
