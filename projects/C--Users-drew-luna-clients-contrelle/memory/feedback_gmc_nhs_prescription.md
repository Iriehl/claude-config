Do not include "Available on NHS prescription." or any NHS prescription reference in GMC product feed titles or descriptions.

**Why:** Causes product approval issues in Google Merchant Centre. Confirmed 2026-04-11.

**How to apply:** When writing or reviewing GMC product titles, descriptions, or any Shopping feed copy for Contrelle — strip any NHS prescription language. It may appear on the website but must not appear in the feed.
