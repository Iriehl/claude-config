## Micro Conversions — Intentional

The "An interaction happens on site" conversion and other proxy micro-conversions are **intentional** — set up to give Google's smart bidding enough signal (~30 conversions/month threshold). Do not flag or recommend removing them. Real contact volume is 90–100/month.

**Why:** Too few real contacts per campaign for smart bidding to optimise effectively without micro-conversion volume signal.
