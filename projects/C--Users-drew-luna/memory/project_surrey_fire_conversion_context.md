## Standing rule — apply to every decision in this account, every session

The Surrey Fire account operates in a **low conversion volume, thin budget** environment. This must inform every recommendation — RSA writing, ad group activation, bid strategy, keyword adds, and campaign changes.

**Why:** User confirmed 2026-05-20. The account struggles for conversions. Smart bidding requires ~30 conversions/campaign/month to exit learning. Micro conversions are running on every campaign to reach that threshold. Account is generating ~90 contact conversions/month total, which means per-campaign signal is low.

**How to apply:**
- Never recommend enabling paused ad groups without flagging the budget dilution and signal dilution impact
- Per-campaign budgets are fixed and thin (Buy = £235 Google, Fire Alarms = £300 Google for 5 groups = £60/group)
- RSA CTAs should be low-friction (free survey, free quote, callback) — not high-commitment
- Avoid structural changes that split conversion signal further (more ad groups = lower signal per group)
- Micro conversions (form micro, page engagement, etc.) are by design — do not remove or pause them
- ~90 contact conversions/month is the current baseline — flag any decision that risks dropping this

**Per-campaign Google Ads budgets (confirmed 2026-05-20):**
- Installation: £703/mo
- Wireless PMax: £750/mo
- Servicing: £312/mo
- Fire Alarms: £300/mo
- Buy: £235/mo
