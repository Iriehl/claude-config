## Key product insights

**Bath mats are the volume backbone, not beach towels.** Mats generate the most conversions and best ROAS. Coordinated mat + pedestal sets consistently appear in top performers - the "complete coordinated look" positioning is backed by actual buying behaviour.

**Beach towels have high AOV (~£200/conversion) but only work in PMax.** In Standard Shopping they run at 43% ROAS (loss-making). They should remain in PMax 20% Best only, not Standard Shopping. The user notes beach towels sell well year-round, not just summer - this contradicts the business.md seasonal note of "May-August summer peak." Monitor for evidence of off-season beach towel demand.
**Why:** Beach towels are PMax's high-value showcase items; Standard Shopping cannot convert them profitably at this spend level.

**Robes/dressing gowns are a standout efficiency play.** Waffle Dressing Gown converted at £5.19 CPA (PMax 80% Other) - the best individual CPA in the account. Multiple variants active (White, Grey, Linen; S/M and L/XL). User confirms these are a major seller. Currently in both PMax campaigns. Below 500% ROAS floor in Standard Shopping - do not push budget there.

**Throws/blankets punch above their weight on AOV.** Waffle Throw and Banjara Sofa Throw both £25-45 products generating £100-175 in combined value. Worth specific asset attention in PMax.

**The "Ooh La La" / novelty slogan range converts.** Scallop Bath Mat (Red/Pink) appeared with 3 convs. Novelty/trend range is not just traffic - it buys.

## Ad copy and asset guidance

- **Lead with coordinated bath set angle** for mat campaigns - "The Complete Bathroom Look" messaging aligns with buying behaviour
- **Robes: use hotel/luxury angle** - "Hotel-Style Waffle Robe" is both honest and effective. These are the most cost-efficient conversions in the account.
- **Beach towels: lean into premium angle** - high AOV means buyers are selecting the best, not cheapest. "100% Cotton Cabana" and specific quality cues matter more than price.
- **Throws: gift and home refresh angle** - Banjara and Waffle Throw both sell well; these are gift-eligible products, Christmas and gifting season copy relevant.
- **Novelty/slogan mats: fun, quick-buy messaging** - short, punchy, specific product names in copy ("Ooh La La Mat" type specificity)

## Campaign routing

- Beach towels: PMax 20% Best only. Not Standard Shopping.
- Robes: PMax is the right vehicle. Watch Standard Shopping robes ROAS - currently 240%, below 500% floor.
- Mats: both PMax 20% Best and Standard Shopping work well. Standard Shopping 787% ROAS.
- Throws/blankets: both channels viable. 407% in Standard Shopping.

**How to apply:** Before writing RSAs, pulling assets, or making campaign structure recommendations - reference this file to focus on the correct product categories and individual winners.
