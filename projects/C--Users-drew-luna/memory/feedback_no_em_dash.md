Never use em dashes (--) in written output.

**Why:** User copies text directly into client-facing documents. Em dashes cause formatting issues or look unprofessional in that context.

**How to apply:** Rewrite any sentence that would naturally use an em dash. Use a comma, colon, semicolon, or restructure the sentence instead. Applies to all output including terminal responses, reports, and emails.
