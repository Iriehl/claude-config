Never audit, recommend LP changes, or flag issues in old legacy campaigns (e.g. [Search] Brand, [Search] Bladder Leaks & Control, [Search] Female Incontinence, [Search] Urinary Problems, [AG] Tena, etc.).

**Why:** These campaigns are not active priorities. Fixing broken LPs, conversion actions, or copy in dormant campaigns is wasted effort. If any old campaign is brought back online, updating LPs and conversion actions is part of the bring-online process — not something to do proactively.

**How to apply:** When pulling account-wide ad data, filter analysis to active new campaigns only: Contrelle - Search, Contrelle - Brand, Contrelle - PMax, Contrelle - Shopping, Contrelle - Demand Gen. Ignore anything prefixed [Search], [AG], [YouTube] or pointing to boots.com unless user specifically asks.
