Don't add recap or summary paragraphs at the end of responses.

**Why:** User finds them redundant — they can read what was done.

**How to apply:** After completing work, stop. Don't write "Here's what I changed: ..." or "In summary: ..." wrap-ups. One final short sentence (what changed + what's next) is fine only when genuinely useful context; otherwise just stop.
